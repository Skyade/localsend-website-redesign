Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free?

Yes, LocalSend is 100% free and open source.
There are no ads, no tracking, and no hidden costs.

Is there any server processing?

No. LocalSend operates entirely on your local network.
Your data never leaves your devices and no external servers are involved in the transfer process.

How secure is it?

Extremely. All transfers are protected by end-to-end encryption using HTTPS,
and you can enable PIN verification for an additional layer of security.

How fast is it?

Incredibly fast. Since it works directly over your local network,
the transfer speed is only limited by your hardware—no cloud bottlenecks or internet lag.
